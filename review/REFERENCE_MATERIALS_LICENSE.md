## General Notes

### J. Hendrik De Vries (1859–1939)

John Hendrik De Vries was a Princeton-educated Presbyterian minister who translated multiple Kuyper works into English, including:
- *Encyclopedia of Sacred Theology* (1898)
- *The Work of the Holy Spirit* (c. 1900)
- *To Be Near Unto God*
- *Lectures on Calvinism* (editorial work)

His translations entered the public domain on **January 1, 2010** (70 years after his death in 1939).

### Modern Reprints

Some of the files in this repository may be derived from modern digital editions (e.g., CCEL, Hendrickson Publishers reprints). While the underlying translation text is public domain, modern editions may include:
- New introductions or annotations (separately copyrighted)
- Typesetting and formatting (generally not copyrightable, but check)
- Editorial notes (may be separately copyrighted)

**Best practice:** Use the plain text versions from CCEL or Archive.org when possible, as these are most likely to be clean public domain texts without modern editorial additions.
